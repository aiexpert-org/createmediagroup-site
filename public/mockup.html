Hi, I'm Emily. I offer unlimited graphic design for churches on a monthly subscription. I would love to help you build toward your vision.

Design intent — what's being mocked

  • Logo: "create." with yellow period (Emily's actual brand mark).
  • Nav active state: yellow marker swipe behind "Home" (replaces the old pill background). Same treatment on hover for every other item.
  • Hero H1: yellow marker swipe behind "graphic design" in the headline.
  • Eyebrow: "CHURCH DESIGN SUBSCRIPTION" in tracking-wide small caps above the H1.
  • CTAs: rectangular 6px radius. Primary yellow + black text, secondary ghost. No more pills.
  • Mosaic background: full-width grid of Emily's actual projects (FCC work for now, more churches as they backfill). Tiles desaturated by default. Move your mouse around — tiles you hover over lift to full color, the rest fade.
  • Gradient overlay: right-to-left fade. White ~96% on the left where the H1 sits, dropping to ~4% on the far right so the colorful work shows through clearly.
  • Brand yellow: #EAD32C (sampled from the wordmark logo file Emily provided).
